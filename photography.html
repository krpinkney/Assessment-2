PHOTOGRAPHY

Photographers help people to see the world in new ways. With the ever-expanding reach of the photographic medium throughout all aspects of our lives, photographers of the future will need a broad critical understanding and highly developed technical skills, together with a flair for adapting to new technologies and modes of communication.

You will be introduced to a range of analogue and digital technologies while developing social and cultural understanding of photography's place in society.
